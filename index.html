Lay-By Policy

  • Minimum 50% deposit required to secure an item on lay-by.
  • Lay-by period is up to 6 weeks unless otherwise agreed.
  • Instalments must be paid on time as agreed at the start.
  • No items released until the full amount has been paid.
  • Lay-by payments are non-refundable. Cancelled = forfeited.
  • Failure to complete payment may result in cancellation.
  • Prices are fixed at time of lay-by — no future price changes.
  • The 10% welcome discount does not apply to lay-by orders.
